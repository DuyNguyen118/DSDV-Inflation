Inflation indicators overview

Inflation is a critical macroeconomic issue that affects economic stability and living standards worldwide, with recent pressures driven by global supply chain disruptions, energy price volatility, and geopolitical tensions. Traditional economic analysis methods often struggle to capture the complex and interconnected nature of inflation across countries, limiting effective policy responses. This project addresses these challenges by developing a data-driven framework to analyze global inflation trends, with a focused case study on Vietnam. The framework transforms raw economic indicators - such as consumer price indices, commodity prices, and exchange rates - into clear, interpretable insights using analytical and visualization techniques. Designed to be accessible and user-friendly, the platform supports policymakers, researchers, and the public in understanding inflation dynamics and making informed economic and policy decisions.
